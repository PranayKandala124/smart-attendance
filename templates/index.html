Per-Student Unique QR

Faculty enters a roll number (e.g. 24B81A3235) and a unique QR is generated. Only that student can use it — logged in as the right roll number.

🚫

Proxy Proof System

QR is tied to roll number + session token + single-use. Screenshots invalid after 60s. Wrong roll = rejected. Different login = rejected.

📍

GPS Campus Lock

Students outside the campus radius cannot mark attendance regardless of QR. Distance calculated with Haversine formula.

⏰

Time-Based Status

Before 9:30 = Present · 9:30–10:00 = Late · After 10:00 = Absent. Automated, no manual intervention.
